Urban beekeeping basics give beginner beekeepers a clear understanding of how to set up safe rooftop hives, select equipment, and keep bees healthy in the city environment.

Rooftop Hive Setup

Rooftop areas provide stable, safe locations for city bees. Make sure the hive is sheltered from strong wind and accessible for regular inspections.

Equipment All Beginners Need

Protective suits, hive tools, smokers, and feeders help new beekeepers manage city hives safely.

Keeping Bees Healthy in the City

Monitor hive activity, offer supplemental feeding during harsh seasons, and avoid areas with heavy pollution.
